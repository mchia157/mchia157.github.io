NCAA Tournament bracket: How UConn, Houston, Purdue
and UNC earned 2024 men’s No. 1 seeds

Selection Sunday began with a pronouncement of the 2024 NCAA Tournament men’s
heavyweight, as Connecticut — aiming to be the first back-to-back national champion since
2007 — was crowned the No. 1 overall seed out of the East.


Other frontrunners Houston (South) and Purdue (Midwest) were also named the No. 1 seeds in
the bracket, with North Carolina (West) snagging the final top seed, edging out Iowa State and
Arizona.
