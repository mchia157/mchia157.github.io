FACTS ABOUT LANGUAGES

1. Mandarin Chinese is considered the most challenging language in the world. It poses a formidable challenge for English speakers, attributed to its tonal pronunciations (four tones), distinctive idioms, and extensive use of logographic characters.

2. English is the language with the most words. This language has about 75000 words, with new ones being added all the time.

3. The United States does not have an official language. English is the most commonly used, but it is not the official language. Over 300 languages are spoken within the country, making it culturally and linguistically diverse.

4. Learning a second language can boost your brain. It comes with benefits like slowing down the brain's ageing process and keeping your mind sharper as you grow older.
