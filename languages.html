LANGUAGES IN THE WORLD

There are 195 countries in the world and the total number of different languages

spoken is impressive. There are about 7139 known languages in the world.

Most Spoken languages in the World

1. English - 1500 millions speakers

2. Mandarin Chinese - 1100 millions speakers

3. Hindi - 609.5 millions speakers

4. Spanish - 559.1 millions speakers

5. French - 309.8 millions speakers

6. Standard Arabic - 274.0 millions speakers

7. Bengali - 272.8 millions speakers

8. Portugese - 263.6 millions speakers

9. Russian - 255.0 millions speakers

10. Urdu - 231.7 millions speakers

Facts about languages

1. Mandarin Chinese is considered the most challenging language in the world.

It poses a formidable challenge for English speakers, attributed to its tonal

pronunciations (four tones), distinctive idioms, and extensive use of logographic characters.

2. English is the language with the most words. This language has about 75000

words, with new ones being added all the time.

3. The United States does not have an official language. English is the most

commonly used, but it is not the official language. Over 300 languages are spoken

within the country, making it culturally and linguistically diverse.

4. Learning a second language can boost your brain. It comes with benefits like

slowing down the brain's ageing process and keeping your mind sharper as you grow

older.
